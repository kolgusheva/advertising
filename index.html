About Kyiv Post

The Kyiv Post is Ukraine's English-language newspaper with with a weekly print circulation of 11,000 copies and an average 5.5 million page views online monthly in 2014. The newspaper was founded in 1995 by American Jed Sunden. In July 2009, British citizen Mohammad Zahoor purchased the newspaper and publishes it through his Public Media company, part of Zahoor's ISTIL Group.

Proud winner of the 2014 Missouri Honor Medal

Kyiv Post received 2014 Missouri Honor Medal for Distinguished Service in Journalism, a lifetime staff  achievement award

Ukraine’s leading English-language publication

During its 20 years in business, Kyiv Post strictly adhered to its motto “Independence. Community. Trust” for the past two decades

History of mentorship and nurturing the talent

Kyiv Post alumni are currently working at leading publications such as Bloomberg, Financial Times, Wall Street Journal,  Mashable. Some went to University and INSEAD
